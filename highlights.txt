1. Location-based identifiers such as URLs and DOIs do not reliably reference digital biodiversity datasets.
2. Between 20%-75% of digital biodiversity datasets in major biodiversity data networks GBIF, iDigBio, DataONE, and BHL changed or were unavailable in period March 2019- May 2020.
3. Cryptographic content-based identifiers provide a reliable method to reference biodiversity datasets.
